## üè• **Healthcare Domain**

### üìä Dataset: [Heart Disease UCI](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/ronitf/heart-disease-uci)

* **Source**: Kaggle / UCI Repository
* **Description**: Predict presence of heart disease based on patient attributes (age, cholesterol, sex, etc.)
* **Potential Problem**: Predictive modeling to detect heart disease risk.
* **CRISP-DM Use**:

  * *Business Understanding*: Early diagnosis for prevention
  * *Data Understanding*: Analyze patient variables
  * *Data Preparation*: Clean nulls, encode categorical values
  * *Modeling*: Logistic Regression, Decision Trees
  * *Evaluation*: ROC/AUC, confusion matrix
  * *Deployment*: Recommend screening policies

## üí∏ **Financial Domain**

### üìä Dataset: [Credit Card Fraud Detection](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/mlg-ulb/creditcardfraud)

* **Source**: Kaggle (European card transactions)
* **Description**: 284,000+ transactions with labels for fraud
* **Potential Problem**: Fraud detection using anomaly detection models
* **CRISP-DM Use**:

  * *Business Understanding*: Minimize fraud loss
  * *Data Understanding*: Explore V1-V28 PCA components
  * *Data Preparation*: Handle class imbalance (SMOTE, under-sampling)
  * *Modeling*: Isolation Forest, Logistic Regression, XGBoost
  * *Evaluation*: Precision/Recall over Accuracy
  * *Deployment*: Real-time fraud alert system

## üè¶ **Insurance Domain**

### üìä Dataset: [Medical Cost Personal Dataset](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/mirichoi0218/insurance)

* **Source**: Kaggle
* **Description**: Predict medical charges based on features like age, BMI, smoking status
* **Potential Problem**: Forecasting individual health insurance premiums
* **CRISP-DM Use**:

  * *Business Understanding*: Price risk-adjusted insurance products
  * *Data Understanding*: Correlations among BMI, smoker, region
  * *Data Preparation*: Normalize numeric features, encode categorical
  * *Modeling*: Linear Regression, Ridge/Lasso
  * *Evaluation*: RMSE, MAE
  * *Deployment*: Premium pricing tool

* **Source**: Kaggle / UCI

* **Potential Problem**: Lead scoring and campaign optimization

  * *Evaluation*: ROC/AUC, F1-score
  * *Deployment*: Build a targeting dashboard for marketers
